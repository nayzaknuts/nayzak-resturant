Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday to Friday from 11am to 9pm, and on Saturdays and Sundays from 12pm to 8pm.

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we do take reservations. You can call us at (123) 456-7890 or use our online reservation form on the Contact page.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan options on our menu, including our delicious hummus and falafel dishes.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Please visit our Contact page to get in touch with us and learn more.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, as well as cash and debit cards.
